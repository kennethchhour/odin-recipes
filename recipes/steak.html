Ingredients

  • Any Steak
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Take steak out of fridge 30 minutes before cooking.
  2. After the steak has come to room temp, pat dry the steak with paper towels.
  3. Turn on stove and heat up some oil in a pan.
  4. Generously season the the steak with salt and pepper on both sides.
  5. Lay steak facing away from you onto the pan and press on the steak for 10 seconds.
  6. After the steak gets a nice sear and flip the steak and do the same for that side.
  7. After both sides have been seared, lower the heat and drop the butter and thyme in the pan.
  8. Start Basting the steak with the butter and thyme.
  9. after about 5 min of basting take the steak out and let it rest for 5-10 minutes.

Voila. A perfect medium-rare steak to eat for dinner.
